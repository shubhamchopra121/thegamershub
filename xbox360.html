Review

In light of the recent software updates, the new Xbox 360 is probably one of the most complete and convenient box's that you can stick under your telly. Not only does it play the latest video games, but it offers a huge amount of other content. Although it lacks having a blu-ray drive, it does come with Zune Video Marketplace which has a lot of 1080p contect which can be streamed or downloaded. The only bad point will be the requirement for Xbox Live Gold for an increasing amount of what's presented on the dashboard. Still, as long as you are the comfortable with the concept of subrscribing to the Gold live membership, it still comes off as a reasonable deal and is by no means mandatory to take advantage of marketplace content. Whether you're a gamer or not, it's time to start considering the Xbox 360 as more than just another place to play the latest Call of Duty or FIFA game.
