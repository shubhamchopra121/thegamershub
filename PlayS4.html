Specification

Review

The PlayStation 4 has always focused on doing one thing incredibly well: playing video games. Two and a half years after hitting store shelves, a few unforeseeable issues have cropped up, but the PS4 is still the best console experience money can buy. They have made a huge improvement with their controllers by adding a more accurate vibration sensor and they have updated the motion sensor which is highly accurate now. The consoles user interface has been updated too. It is much more modern and more simple to use. Although, it still carries a hefty price tag, it is so worth having.
