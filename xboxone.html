Review

Far better than it once was, and destined to keep improving, Xbox One is a truly modern piece of kit. It suffers for its catch-all approach a little, but you won't see some of its tricks on any competitor's machine. The positives for the xbox one are;- Excellent game selection Great online service The best controller going Unmatched media features Able to evolve constantly However, the negatives are;- Inferior graphics Confusing UI without Kinect Poor sharing options Ugly unit design The Xbox One is now a true multimedia hub, close to what Microsoft always promised it would be. It's a next-gen games console, a TV companion and guide (compatible with Sky, Virgin, BT, Freeview and Freesat), a media streaming centre, a Skype phone, a Blu-ray player, a pizza delivery machine and more.
